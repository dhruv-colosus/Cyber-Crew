About

“The limits of the possible can only be defined by going beyond them into the impossible. The world is full of magical things patiently waiting for our wits to grow sharper.”
Success comes to those who dedicate everything to their passion in life. Determination, zeal and perseverance script a story which soars beyond the realms of a fairy tale, to inspire generations which have gone by or will live to relate. The precincts of Jaipuria, have since inception provided a promising arena to all those students who would like to unveil, contour and sharpen their innate talents and augment their potential. It is enticing all the future globe trotters to partake by providing a nurturing atmosphere to join forces, develop and foster talents and values.
“Compufest” is a Jaipurian legacy; a passionate two days extravaganza visited by multitude of young ignited superstars bubbling with palpable energy and soaring spirits. A plethora of dazzling, nerve wracking and exhilarating activities astound the young enthusiastic feisty minds, whose sheer brilliance and competitive spirits foster collaboration.
It is indeed our privilege to invite the multi-talented, dexterous scholars to participate in “Compufest-2020”-The Techno-Cultural Fest. It aims to encourage computing, cutting edge technology, creativity and ingenuity to make a quantum leap into the future. This collaborative confluence of students from different schools assists to unearth inherent aptitude, imagination and manifests intrinsic abilities. An array of divergent events ranging from Decode-X,Sherlocked,Lets Talk,Virtual Warriors etc. shall provide a platform for the techno-savvy generation which needs no spotlight, however can radiate better with some impetus that shall be provided by the Jaipurian premises.
